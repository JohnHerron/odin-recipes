Fruit and Yogurt Smoothie

Description

This pink swirl of delectable fruits and yogurt combines together to create a jaw dropping mid-day dessert. Not only does it taste delicious, but it boasts numerous health benefits due to its high fruit content a nutritional value.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup strawberries
  • 1 banana
  • ½ cup yogurt
  • ¼ cup pineapple juice
  • 1 ½ teaspoons white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon orange juice
  • 1 teaspoon milk

Steps

  1. Prepare all your ingredients ahead of time
  2. Put all your fruits, yogurt, juice, and sugar in the blender
  3. Simply blend until smooth!
